Programs

  • Day 1 : Cape Spear - Stand here with your back to the sea and the entire population of North America is to the west of you. Face the sea and the next stop east is Ireland.
  • Day 2 : Signal Hill - There are several hiking trails at Signal Hill National Historic Site, all of which boast panoramic views and captivating scenery. Bring appropriate footwear.
  • Day 3 : Downtown - Iceberg Quest has been showcasing Newfoundland and Labrador for ten years. It’s a place where the Atlantic Ocean meets the edge of the earth.
  • Day 4 : Terra Nova - Enjoy a 12 minute orientation video of the park or uncover the mysteries of the ocean by visiting the touch tank, aquarium and underwater camera exhibits, or take an mp3 tour of the heritage trail.

Services Included

  • Hotel Transition - Each and every night you’ll retreat to the best hotels in the region.
  • 3 course meals for the entire stay - All breakfasts and most lunches and dinners are included in the price of your trip.
  • Trip Insurance - We even offer trip insurance that you may purchase to protect you just in case.
  • Transportation Included - On our luxury trips we provide transportation both at the beginning and at the end of your trip .

Services Excluded

  • Air Ticket - Airfare to and from the trip
  • Occasional Meals - Some meals are not included in each destination, which allows you the opportunity to explore regional cuisine on your own.
  • Tips - Gratuities for your Trek Travel guides are not included in your trip price. Industry standard is 5-10% of your trip price for the guide team.
  • Basic - Lodging before and after the trip, Transportation to the trip pick-up location
  • No alcholic beverages offered
